MLB Live Game Monitor — Changelog
[1.4.0] — 2025‑08‑10 (ROI Alerts Pack)
Added
AI: Power Hitter + Scoring Opportunity (checkbox)

Triggers when a batter with a high Power Alert Score (internal 0–100) is on deck or at‑bat and there’s a high‑leverage run opportunity (e.g., RISP, ≤2 outs, tie/one‑run game, late innings).

Includes park, weather (wind out/temperature), platoon split, pitcher HR susceptibility, and recent barrels.

Priority: High. Dedup window: 90s, immediate re‑alert on tier upgrade (B→A).

AI: Power Hitter — High‑Confidence (checkbox)

Fires on Tier A Power Alert Score (≥70) regardless of RISP, but gated to on‑deck/at‑bat only.

Optimized for quick swing props/HR markets and “to record XBH” spots.

Priority: High. Dedup window: 90s.

Pitcher Softening (Fatigue/Contact) Check (checkbox)

Detects pitcher deterioration using:

3rd time through order (TTO≥3),

Last two innings hard‑hit%↑ or EV↑,

HR/9 last 30d ≥ 75th percentile or rising trend within game (FB%↑/miss↓),

Pitch count over threshold (default 75/90 for SPs) and velo drop ≥ 1.0 mph from inning 1 average (if available).

Publishes when ≥2 conditions confirm softening.

Priority: Medium–High. Dedup window: 120s.

Pitcher: 8+ Balls in a Row (checkbox)

Fires when any pitcher throws 8 consecutive balls (walks/near loss of command).

Adds context: last two PAs outcomes, bullpen status (if known), base/out state.

Priority: High (immediate betting implications on live totals/walk props). Dedup window: 60s; re‑alert at 10 and 12 balls.

Changed
Monitor → Choose What to Monitor gained a new AI Alerts section:

☐ AI: Power Hitter + Scoring Opportunity

☐ AI: Power Hitter — High‑Confidence

☐ Pitcher Softening (Fatigue/Contact)

☐ Pitcher: 8+ Balls in a Row

Alert payloads now include:

score, tier, why[] for AI power hitter alerts,

softening_reasons[] for pitcher fatigue,

ball_streak for command loss,

roi_hint with a short suggestion like "HR/XTBR", "Live total over", or "Opp team TT over".

Fixed
Dedup keys upgraded to include tier + rounded score + inning so upgrades surface immediately without spam.

Race condition on quick state flips (on_deck → at_bat) no longer drops the alert.

UI labels (for settings panel):

Alert Definitions (for reference)
AI: Power Hitter + Scoring Opportunity

Gate: state ∈ {on_deck, at_bat} AND RISP=True OR (Leverage≥0.7).

Condition: PowerScore≥55 (Tier B+) OR Tier A.

Priority hint: roi_hint="HR/XTBR or opp TT over if softening".

AI: Power Hitter — High‑Confidence

Gate: state ∈ {on_deck, at_bat}.

Condition: PowerScore≥70 (Tier A).

Priority hint: roi_hint="HR, XBH, TB over".

Pitcher Softening

Signals: (TTO≥3) + (EV↑ or HardHit%↑) + (HR/9_30d_high OR velo_drop≥1.0mph OR pitch_count_high).

Fire when signals_met ≥ SOFTEN_MIN_SIGNALS.

Priority hint: roi_hint="Live over, opp TT, next PA hit props".

8+ Balls in a Row

Condition: ball_streak ≥ 8 (re‑alert at 10,12).

Priority hint: roi_hint="Walk props, live over, opp TT".

Dedup Keys & Windows (implementation note)
Base: f"{game_id}_{alert_type}_{subject_id}"

Content key:

Power hitter: f"{tier}_{round(score)}_{pitcher_id}_{inning}"

Softening: f"{signals_met}_{tto}_{inning}"

Ball streak: f"{ball_streak}_{inning}"

Windows: A/B=90s, Softening=120s, BallStreak=60s.

Upgrade rule: If content key changes to a higher severity (e.g., B→A or 8→10 balls), bypass window and alert immediately.

Logging & QA
New fields in alerts.log: components, softening_reasons, ball_streak.

Sampled telemetry (1/10 alerts) records pre‑filter candidates for post‑hoc ROI tuning.

Migration Notes
No DB schema migration required; config adds safe defaults.

Ensure env has weather/park inputs available for AI Power alerts; if missing, model degrades gracefully with neutral weights.
